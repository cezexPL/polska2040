# Poland 2040: Technology Sovereignty — Executive Brief

## Executive Summary

- **Poland should build an implementation system, not a single drone programme.** Operational demand, data, testing, procurement, production, talent and export must form one measurable cycle.
- **Technology sovereignty is resilience, not autarky.** Critical interfaces, supplier substitution and crisis production matter more than domestic production of every component.
- **Polish scale and EU/NATO access can complement Ukrainian operational learning.** Partnerships require strict ownership, security, export-control and continuity safeguards.

## The strategic choice

Establish a cross-government portfolio for autonomous systems, AI and dual-use technologies with clear owners, rapid pilots and independent evaluation.
